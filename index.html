Projects

Sound Sensor Car

Developed an Arduino-based car model integrated with sound sensors and a buzzer to detect unusual or loud noises in the surrounding environment. This project demonstrates the working principles of anti-theft alert systems in vehicles, highlighting the practical applications of sound detection, sensor technology, and embedded electronics. By building and programming the system, we were able to explore how real-time data from sensors can trigger automatic responses, providing a hands-on understanding of the interaction between hardware, software, and practical security solutions in modern automotive technology.

College Club Website

This project is primarily developed for our PRG-303 module, where our team of five programmers collaborated to design and implement the system. The main objective of the project is to create a unified online platform that allows students to access all essential information and services in one convenient location. Through this platform, students can stay updated with the latest announcements, join clubs, participate in various events, and utilize club-related services seamlessly. By integrating multiple functionalities into a single system, the project aims to simplify communication, enhance student engagement, and provide a centralized digital solution that makes campus life more organized and accessible.

Crypto Token

This is a crypto token that I developed in collaboration with two of my friends as part of our effort to explore blockchain technology and its practical applications. The token is designed to function as a digital currency, enabling us to trade and make online transactions without the need to rely on traditional financial systems or government-backed currencies. One of the most exciting aspects of this project is the incentive mechanism we built into the system: whenever others use our token for transactions, I earn a small transaction fee. This not only creates a passive income stream but also provides a practical demonstration of how decentralized finance (DeFi) works in real life. Through this project, I have gained valuable knowledge about blockchain networks, smart contracts, and digital asset management, while at the same time creating something of real value that can grow with adoption. It has been a rewarding experience that combines technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
